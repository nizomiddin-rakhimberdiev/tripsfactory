Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Arrival in Tashkent - The Gateway
expand_more

Welcome to Uzbekistan! Upon arrival at Tashkent International Airport, you will be met by our representative and transferred to your luxury hotel. After a short rest, enjoy a curated welcome dinner at a traditional manor, featuring the famous 'Tashkent Plov' and live folk music. Your guide will provide a detailed briefing of the upcoming journey through the Silk Road cities.

Day 2-3 Khiva - The Open Air Museum
expand_more

Fly to Urgench and drive to the ancient fortress city of Khiva. Spend two days exploring Itchan Kala, the inner city surrounded by massive mudbrick walls. Highlights include the Kalta Minor Minaret and the Pakhlavan Mahmoud Mausoleum.

Day 4-6 Bukhara - The Noble City
expand_more

A journey across the desert brings you to Bukhara. Here, time seems to have stopped. You'll visit the Ark Fortress, the Poi-Kalyan complex, and the famous trading domes where silk and spice merchants still gather.
